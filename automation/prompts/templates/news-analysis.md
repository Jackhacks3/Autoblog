# News Analysis Template

## Article Type
Timely analysis of industry news, product announcements, or market developments with expert commentary.

## Target Audience
Industry professionals, executives, and informed observers who want deeper insight beyond headlines.

## Required Elements

### Structure
1. **News Hook** (50-100 words)
   - State the news clearly and concisely
   - Include key facts: who, what, when
   - Establish why this matters

2. **Context & Background** (150-200 words)
   - Relevant history leading to this announcement
   - Key players involved
   - Market conditions or trends that set the stage

3. **What Happened** (200-300 words)
   - Detailed breakdown of the announcement/development
   - Key features, changes, or implications
   - Official statements or data points

4. **Analysis: Why It Matters** (300-400 words)
   - Expert interpretation of significance
   - Impact on different stakeholders
   - Comparison to competitors or alternatives
   - Potential second-order effects

5. **Industry Reactions** (100-150 words)
   - Summarize expert opinions
   - Note any controversy or debate
   - Include balanced perspectives

6. **What's Next** (100-150 words)
   - Timeline for rollout or implementation
   - Upcoming milestones to watch
   - Questions that remain unanswered

7. **Key Takeaways** (bullet list)
   - 3-5 actionable insights
   - What readers should do with this information

## Tone Adjustments
- Objective and balanced (present multiple viewpoints)
- Timely language ("today," "this week," "just announced")
- Analytical rather than promotional
- Confident but not overly speculative

## Word Count
800-1,200 words

## Timeliness Requirements
- Reference specific dates when relevant
- Acknowledge if analysis is based on limited initial information
- Note any expected updates or follow-up announcements

## Example Topics
- "Claude 4 Announcement: What It Means for Enterprise AI"
- "OpenAI's Latest Release: Breaking Down the Key Changes"
- "New SEC Crypto Regulations: Impact Analysis for Digital Asset Firms"
