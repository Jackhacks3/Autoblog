# Market Analysis Template

## Article Type
Data-driven analysis of market trends, competitive landscapes, or investment themes in technology and digital assets.

## Target Audience
Investors, analysts, business strategists, and professionals tracking market dynamics.

## Required Elements

### Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (100-150 words)
   - Key findings in 3-4 bullet points
   - Bottom-line assessment
   - Who should read this and why

2. **Market Overview** (200-300 words)
   - Current market size and growth trajectory
   - Key players and market share
   - Recent significant developments

3. **Data Analysis** (300-400 words)
   - Present key metrics and trends
   - Include specific numbers and percentages
   - Compare year-over-year or period-over-period
   - Highlight anomalies or inflection points

4. **Competitive Landscape** (200-300 words)
   - Major players and their positioning
   - Recent competitive moves
   - Emerging challengers or disruptors

5. **Trend Analysis** (250-350 words)
   - Identify 3-5 key trends shaping the market
   - Explain drivers behind each trend
   - Assess sustainability and trajectory

6. **Risk Factors** (150-200 words)
   - Key risks to the market or thesis
   - Regulatory considerations
   - Technology or adoption risks

7. **Outlook & Predictions** (150-200 words)
   - Short-term outlook (6-12 months)
   - Medium-term trajectory (1-3 years)
   - Key catalysts to watch

8. **Key Takeaways** (bullet list)
   - 4-6 actionable insights
   - Specific recommendations by reader type

## Tone Adjustments
- Analytical and data-driven
- Balanced and objective (acknowledge uncertainty)
- Precise with numbers and timeframes
- Avoid hype or promotional language
- Use conditional language for predictions ("may," "could," "if")

## Word Count
1,000-1,500 words

## Data Requirements
- Include specific metrics and statistics
- Cite sources for data claims
- Use comparison frameworks (YoY, market share, growth rates)
- Present data visually where possible (describe charts/tables)

## Example Topics
- "State of Enterprise AI Adoption: 2026 Market Analysis"
- "Tokenization Market: Growth Drivers and Barriers"
- "LLM Provider Landscape: Comparing the Major Players"
